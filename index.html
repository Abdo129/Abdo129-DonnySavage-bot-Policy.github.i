DonnySavage Bot Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2023-11-15

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how DonnySavage Bot ("the Bot") collects, uses, and protects your information when you interact with it on Discord.

2. Data Collection

We collect minimal data necessary for moderation:

  • Server IDs: For configuration storage
  • User IDs: For moderation tracking
  • Message Metadata: Author, timestamp, channel
  • Attachment URLs: Temporary links to images (PNG/JPEG/GIF only)

We never store actual file content or personal information.

3. How We Use Data

Data is used exclusively for:

  • Moderation and safety enforcement
  • Bot functionality (scheduled messages, welcome systems)
  • Debugging and improvement

4. Data Retention

  • Message data: 14 days maximum
  • Attachment URLs: 48 hours maximum
  • Server configurations: Until bot removal

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • View your data with /request-my-data
  • Delete your data with /delete-my-data
  • Adjust preferences with /privacy

6. Contact Us

For questions or data requests:

  • In-app: Use /report command
  • Discord: Join our Support Server
  • Email: privacy@donnysavagebot.com
